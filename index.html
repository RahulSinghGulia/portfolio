About Me

I am a passionate researcher and educator specializing in wireless communication, edge intelligence, and generative AI for future networks. My work bridges physical-layer modeling with AI-driven optimization, enabling real-time, resilient, and energy-efficient wireless systems for industrial and defense applications.

Research Highlights

Memory-Augmented Generative AI for Real-time Wireless Prediction in Dynamic Industrial Environments

This project introduces a hybrid AI framework combining generative memory modules with predictive wireless modeling. It enables real-time SINR and channel prediction in non-stationary, interference-rich environments — advancing digital-twin-based industrial communication.

  • Combines generative AI and memory augmentation for wireless prediction tasks.
  • Demonstrates fast convergence and robustness in industrial UWB/5G settings.
  • Potential applications in autonomous manufacturing and UAV networks.
